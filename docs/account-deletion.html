Delete Your Account

This page explains how to delete your Only2U account within the app and what gets removed.

In-App Deletion (Recommended) No support contact needed

  1. Open the Only2U app and sign in (if not already).
  2. Tap your profile avatar or name to open your Profile.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Scroll to the Account section and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your identity (you may be asked to verify via OTP).
  6. Review what will be deleted and tap Delete to proceed.

You can cancel before the final confirmation. After deletion starts, the process is irreversible.

What’s Deleted?

  • Your profile details (name, email, phone, location, skin tone).
  • Your wishlist and app preferences.
  • Your app session/authentication data.

Some order information may be retained where legally required (e.g., invoices). We remove personal identifiers from retained records wherever possible.

Timeline & Verification

  • Deletion is typically completed within 7 days.
  • We may ask for OTP verification to ensure the request came from the account owner.
  • We’ll notify you in-app or via email once deletion is complete.

Can’t Access the App?

If you can’t use the in-app option (e.g., lost number), you can request deletion via email.

Email Support to Delete Request via Form

Include the phone number used for OTP login and your full name to help us verify ownership.

FAQs

Can I recover my account after deletion?
No. Account deletion is permanent. You can create a new account later using the same phone number if it’s available.

Will my orders be deleted?
We may retain non-identifiable order records for legal and accounting purposes. Personal identifiers are removed whenever possible.

What if I made a deletion request by mistake?
If the request hasn’t been processed yet, contact support immediately and we’ll try to stop it.
