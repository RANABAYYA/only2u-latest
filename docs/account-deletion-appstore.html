‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I reactivate my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent. You'll need to create a new account with a different email address. The same phone number can be used after 30 days.

What happens to my pending orders?

You must wait for all pending orders to be completed or cancel them before deletion. Orders in transit cannot be deleted until delivered or returned.

Will my reviews be deleted?

Reviews will be anonymized (shown as "Anonymous User") rather than deleted to maintain product rating integrity. Your identity is removed but the review content remains.

Can I recover my coin balance?

No. Coin balances cannot be converted to money or refunded. Please spend your coins before requesting deletion.

What if I change my mind?

You have 7 days to cancel your deletion request by emailing support@only2u.com with subject "Cancel Account Deletion Request". After 7 days, deletion is irreversible.

How long does complete deletion take?

Immediate: Account deactivation
7 days: Active data removal
30 days: Complete system deletion
90 days: Backup deletion

What if I can't access the app?

Send an email to support@only2u.com with your registered email and phone number. We'll verify your identity and process the deletion manually.

Will vendors I bought from still have my information?

Vendors' copies of fulfilled orders may be retained per their own policies, but Only2U will anonymize your information in our systems.
