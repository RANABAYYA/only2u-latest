## üöÄ CORE FEATURES

### 1. **AI Virtual Try-On** (Star Feature)
**The Game Changer:** Users upload their photo once and can instantly see themselves wearing any outfit in the catalog.

**Technology:**
- **Face Swap AI** (PiAPI & Akool Integration)
- Upload profile photo ‚Üí AI swaps user's face onto product model images
- **Photo Preview:** 4 coins ‚Üí Get 3 AI-generated images showing you in the outfit
- **Video Preview:** 10 coins ‚Üí Get AI-generated video of you wearing the outfit
- **Real-time Processing:** 5-30 seconds with live progress indicators
- **Multiple Results:** View different angles and styles
- **Coin System:** Gamified with virtual coins for try-ons

**User Flow:**
1. Browse products in Trending/Dashboard
2. Tap "Try On" button
3. Select Photo (4 coins) or Video (10 coins) preview
4. AI processes in real-time
5. View yourself in the outfit instantly
6. Save, share, or add to cart
